ABOUT

M-Cal Inc is an award-winning residential and commercial heating & cooling company with 30 years of experience servicing around 30,000 residential & commercial customers in the Greater Toronto Region & Belleville area.

M-Cal was founded by Michael Clamusa in the year 1990 with the mission of providing innovative and high-quality HVAC solutions. Customer satisfaction is one of our most important priorities.

Our success did not happen overnight but through several innovative changes in the way we do business and by ensuring customer satisfaction.Our service & installation department is open 24/7 a day 365 days a year, we never charge extra for nights, weekends.
